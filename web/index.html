Features

WHAT IT DOES

A proper biomechanics analyzer, not just a rep counter.

AUTO CALIBRATION

Stands up and squats once at startup — automatically sets thresholds for your body and camera angle. No manual config.

3D ANGLE MATH

Uses MediaPipe's z-depth coordinates and dot product formula. Accurate knee angle at any camera position — even diagonal.

FORM WARNINGS

Detects round back (shoulder/hip angle) and knee cave-in (3D lateral deviation) in real-time with color-coded alerts.

AUTO LEG SELECTION

Compares MediaPipe visibility scores for both legs and always tracks the one the camera can see better.

33 KEYPOINTS

Full MediaPipe Pose model tracks 33 body landmarks including hands, face and feet — full skeleton overlay on video.

VIDEO FILE SUPPORT

Works with pre-recorded videos as well as live webcam. Useful for reviewing form from a recorded workout session.
